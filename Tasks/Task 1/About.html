About Us - Payactiv

At Payactiv, we believe that financial wellness is the foundation for a healthier, more productive, and motivated workforce. Founded with the mission to empower individuals with timely access to earned wages, Payactiv offers a holistic solution to break the cycle of financial stress and paycheck-to-paycheck living.

Our Story

Payactiv was created with a vision to provide working people with real-time access to the money they've already earned, but too often can't access until payday. Since our inception, we've become a leader in Earned Wage Access (EWA), helping millions of employees manage their cash flow and avoid costly debt traps such as payday loans, late fees, and overdraft charges. By partnering with employers, Payactiv brings financial dignity and flexibility to the workplace.

What We Do

We offer a range of financial tools designed to give employees control over their finances:

  • Earned Wage Access: Immediate access to earned wages before payday.
  • Savings and Budgeting Tools: Helping individuals save for the future and manage their spending.
  • Bill Payments: Simplified and integrated bill payment options.
  • Discounted Services: Special deals on everyday expenses like groceries, prescriptions, and rideshare services.
  • Financial Counseling: Guidance on managing money, building credit, and planning for financial goals.
