Use Of Section Tag (Works same as paragraph tag.)

The sectionelement defines a section in a document.According to W3C's HTML documentation: "A section is a thematic grouping of content, typically with a heading." Examples of where a section element can be used:Chapters,Introduction,News items,Contact information.A web page could normally be split into sections for introduction, content, and contact information.


The Article Element

What Does Article Do?

Article Use:

The article element specifies independent, self-contained content.An article should make sense on its own, and it should be possible to distribute it independently from the rest of the web site. Examples of where the article element can be used:Forum posts,Blog posts,User comments,Product cards,Newspaper articles


Aside Tag Use

The aside element defines some content aside from the content it is placed in (like a sidebar).The aside content should be indirectly related to the surrounding content.
