Fauji Fertilizer Bin Qasim Limited

Fauji Fertilizer Bin Qasim Limited (FFBL) is mainly involved in manufacturing of chemical fertilizers for the agriculture sector of Pakistan.

FFBL is the only manufacturer of DAP and Granular Urea in Pakistan.
